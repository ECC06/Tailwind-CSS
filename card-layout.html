My favourite animals

Red Panda

A small, tree-dwelling mammal native to the Himalayas with reddish-brown fur and a bushy ringed tail. Despite its name, it's not closely related to giant pandas but is more similar to raccoons. Red pandas are excellent climbers and feed mainly on bamboo.

Sea Otter

A charismatic marine mammal found along the coasts of the Pacific Ocean. Sea otters are known for using rocks as tools to crack open shellfish while floating on their backs. They have the densest fur of any animal, with up to a million hairs per square inch.

Grizzly Bear

The grizzly bear is a large subspecies of brown bear native to North America, primarily found in Alaska, western Canada, and parts of the northwestern United States. These powerful predators can weigh between 400-800 pounds (with males being significantly larger than females) and stand up to 8 feet tall when on their hind legs.

Fennec Fox

The world's smallest fox species, native to the Sahara Desert. Fennec foxes have enormous ears (up to 6 inches long) that help them dissipate heat and locate prey underground. Their cream-colored fur helps them blend into their sandy environment.
